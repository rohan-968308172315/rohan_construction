About Us

We Are Professional Construction Company Since 2008

Rohan Construction is a leading construction company based in Ahmednagar, Maharashtra. With over 15 years of experience in the industry, we have successfully completed numerous residential, commercial, and industrial projects.

Our team of skilled professionals is committed to delivering high-quality construction services while maintaining strict safety standards and adhering to project timelines.

We take pride in our attention to detail, innovative approach, and commitment to customer satisfaction. Our goal is to transform your vision into reality with precision and excellence.
